# Debt <Badge type="warning" text="Preview Content" />

Debt occurs when a [Signed Merchant Effigy](../items/merchant-effigy#giving-effigies)
activates and spawns the [Chains](../entities/chains). In the preview, this is the only 
way to get into debt, and you will be in debt permanently to the giver of the effigy.

## Effects
While in debt, the giver can do the following:
- Freely loot your inventory via [Chains](../entities/chains).
- Take your [Arm](../items/arm).
- [Blind you](../items/contract#usage).
- [Rename you](../blocks/pawn#using-a-nametag).
