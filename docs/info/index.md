# Information & Lore

Charter was made for Content SMP (CSMP), a Survival Multiplayer server where the participants get to
add in their own content to enhance the gameplay and interactions. Most of the storyline is
scripted, however, not all of it is per se "fake", as in the end, it's still an SMP they built
original things on.

Charter plays a special role in a lot of parts of the story of CSMP. In this
section of the wiki, we dive deeper into the lore bits that have to do with Charter
while also looking at some of the backstory of how the mod came to its state now, and
what the future entails.
